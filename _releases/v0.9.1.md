Hotfix for a navigation regression introduced in v0.9.0.

## Fixes

### [INVOS-88] Fund Settings cards no longer redirect to the dashboard
After the v0.9.0 URL restructuring (every fund-scoped page now lives under `/:slug/…`), the Fund Settings landing cards — Stages, Application Forms, Users, Asset Types, Custom Fields, Active Locks — navigated to slug-less paths (`/settings/stages`). The router couldn't match those and bounced the user straight to the dashboard, making the entire Settings area unreachable. The cards now carry the active fund slug, so each one opens its section correctly.
