## Features

### [INVOS-35] Attachments on the Company Profile
The Company Profile tab now has an **Attachments** section where you can add one or more files (pitch decks, reports, any document) directly to a company. Files are stored in the company's Drive under a `profile/` folder, so they're available both on the profile and in the Drive. The new field is also available as a draggable **Attachments** field in **Fund Settings → Application Forms**, so applicants can submit files through your application forms.

### [INVOS-37] Upload a Company Logo
You can now set a company's logo from the Profile tab — just click the logo and choose an image. It's stored in the company's Drive (`profile/logo`) and shows wherever the company appears. Previously companies could only show an auto-migrated logo or initials.

### [INVOS-38] Fund Logo
Funds can now have their own logo, set from **Fund Settings**. The logo (favicon-style) appears in the in-app fund selector, on the Funds admin list, and on the **public application form** header for that fund — giving each fund consistent branding for applicants. The image is downscaled automatically and kept lightweight.

## Fixes

### [INVOS-30] Deal Team remarks were not being saved
On a company's **Deal** tab, entering Remarks and clicking Save appeared to work, but the text was gone after a refresh — it was silently discarded because there was nowhere to store it. Remarks now persist correctly and are shown whenever you return to the screen.

### [INVOS-32] Deal Currency and Fund Currency dropdowns were out of sync
The **Deal Currency** (company) and **Fund Currency** (fund settings) dropdowns showed different currency lists and orderings, which was confusing. They now draw from a single canonical currency list, so every currency dropdown across funds and deals shows the same options in the same order.

**Run incremental migration after pulling new images — no data re-seed required:**
```bash
docker compose exec inf-backend sh -c "cd /app && npx knex migrate:latest"
docker compose restart inf-backend
```

- `011_company_remarks` — adds `companies.remarks` (Deal tab remarks)
- `012_fund_logo` — adds `funds.logo_base64` (inline fund logo)
